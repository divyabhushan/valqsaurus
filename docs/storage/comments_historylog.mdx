## Comments

### Add comments for Planning

<div style={{textAlign: 'justify'}}>
To add a comment for Plan tab, select the cell you want to comment on and then click the comment option.
After posting, your comment is then saved to the comment section for Planning under the planning series and node hierarchy alongside
your username and time stamp.
</div> <br /> 

<div style={{textAlign: 'center'}}>
  <Zoom>
<img alt="" src={useBaseUrl("/doc-images//sc5.7.2.png")} />
  </Zoom>
</div>

### Add comments for Simulation

<div style={{textAlign: 'justify'}}>
On the Simulate canvas, you can comment on the changes you have made by clicking the comment
icon on top of the node which appears as you hover over the node. After posting, your comment is then saved to the comment section for Simulation under the scenario name and node hierarchy
alongside your username and time stamp.
</div> <br /> 

<div style={{textAlign: 'center'}}>
  <Zoom>
<img alt="" src={useBaseUrl("/doc-images//sc5.7.1.png")} />
  </Zoom>
</div> <br />

<div style={{textAlign: 'justify'}}>
Alternatively, you can check the comment log for the entire model by
clicking the comments icon at the bezel. Comments are displayed at the
node level for Planning and Simulation.
</div> <br />

<div style={{textAlign: 'center'}}>

### View, Edit, Delete Comments

<div style={{textAlign: 'justify'}}>
The comments can be edited anytime by using the pencil icon next to your name. The bin icon can be used to delete a comment permanently.
The users part of the same workspace will be able to view the comments made by other members and can add their comments to the model.
</div>

## History Log

<div style={{textAlign: 'justify'}}>

As you go to the 3 dots `...` at the bezel and click History Log, you can see the 
changes made to the model in chronological order for Planning and Simulation. This
feature helps with auditing the ValQ model.
</div>
