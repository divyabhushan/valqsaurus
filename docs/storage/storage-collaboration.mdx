## Upload ValQ model to your Workspace

<div style={{ textAlign: "justify" }}>
  Uploading the model facilities various collaboration functionalities such as
  saving the model to a workspace, sharing your model, adding comments,
  conducting audit trail, sharing scenarios, model merge and exporting the model
  as a data source to be used for other visuals or further analysis.
</div>
<br />

Here are the steps to upload the ValQ model to your workspace

### 1. Log in to workspace

<div style={{ textAlign: "justify" }}>
  Log in to your workspace at the top right corner to see the Upload button and
